Som terms we need to get right considering weapons:
	BULLET		- the piece of metal that flies from the barren when you shoot
	ROUND		- the whole package that goes into gun's chamber. the BULLET, case, gunpowder and primer
	MAG			- see MAGAZINE
	MAGAZINE	- metal or plastic container for the ROUNDs that allows feeding them to the chamber to be shot
	CLIP		- usually a metal ROUND holder used for rapid refilling of MAGAZINEs
